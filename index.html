We exist to improve the student experience, and we do this by running sports clubs, supporting student societies, putting on student events, providing bars, cafes, shops, jobs, and a lot more - all for our students!.

We have a handful of bars on campus, whether you're looking for a chilled drink with friends after lectures, somewhere to watch the football or somewhere to have a dance, we’ve got you covered! All of our prices are student friendly but keep your eyes peeled for our Happy Hour deals too!

We have three physical shops on our campus, the Union Shop, situated down at Falmer House with the rest of our SU services, the Southern Co-operative, located up at Bramber House, right in the middle of campus, and Union - the Sussex branded merchandise Shop, located in the arches next to the new Student Centre. We've also got an online shop where you'll find the memorabilia you’re after.

The Student Union advice team provide advice on University-related issues and housing for every Sussex student. If it’s not an area we cover then we can point you in the right direction to get help.
